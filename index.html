How do I register?
On the website’s homepage, there is a “Registration” button that opens a form when clicked. You need to fill out all the fields carefully. The information you provide is sent for manual moderation.
How do I get a personal manager?
After registration, no later than the end of the next working day, the manager will contact you using your provided contact information. How do I connect an offer? All information about offers is available in the “Offers” tab, where you can get acquainted with everything and choose the most suitable option, after which you can leave a ticket to connect. Contact your personal manager if you do not know where to direct your traffic. We will always suggest the most converting offers with maximum benefits for you.
How often is statistics updated?
Click statistics are updated in real-time. Conversion can be displayed instantly or with a delay (from 15 minutes to a day), depending on the specific advertiser’s system operation features.
What is a Hold?
A hold is the period required for the advertiser to analyze the quality of leads before payment is made.
What is a “cap” (Test Cap)?
When you start working with an offer, you need to go through a qualification process so that the advertiser recognizes your traffic as high quality. To do this, a certain number of conversions must be generated on the offer in test mode, referred to as the test limit. The test cap is determined and set under the rules of the specific advertiser.
How can I get a higher rate?
Starting rates can be revised after the test period. Also, it is essential to remember that high traffic volumes (with good quality) are the main reason for rates to increase.
How is traffic quality evaluated?
The advertiser evaluates traffic activity on the platform, repeat visits, CR from registrations to paid actions, repeat conversions, time spent on landing pages, bounce rates, and many other indicators. These make up the offer’s KPI.
When does the advertiser conduct a reconciliation?
Reconciliations occur on different schedules for different advertisers: weekly, twice a month, or monthly. You can find out more details from your personal manager. When can I receive payment? You should contact your personal manager for this question, as the payment terms vary for all offers.
How can I receive early payment?
Early payments are only available at the discretion of the personal manager to proven webmasters who have successfully passed the test cap.
What is the minimum payout amount?
Our CPA-network is ready to pay from 1 lead, the minimum payment from 100$ but it should be noted that some advertisers pay for a certain number of leads (2-5 and more).
Where can I receive payment?
The main payment system for making payments in our PP is USDT. Payments to this payment system are made without additional fees. Withdrawals to other payment systems are possible; please check with your personal manager for details.
How can I change my payment details and method?
There is a “Profile” tab in your personal account, then “Payment system,” where you can set the necessary payment details. Ask your manager to set the required details.
What is meant by “prohibited traffic”?
Prohibited traffic is traffic that will not be paid for by the advertiser. The list of banned traffic includes:
Fraud, Bots and Motivated Traffic” are considered sources of fraudulent traffic on advertising platforms, including active advertising systems and pay-to-click services. Cashback traffic is also prohibited from promising users money or other rewards through payments to webmasters. Using bots, multiple accounts, or any form of user manipulation is not permitted. Cheating schemes involving casinos are also prohibited, except in those offers where they are officially permitted.
The advertiser conducts a traffic check, evaluating many behavioral factors and profitability to determine fraudulent traffic. These indicators include platform activity, repeat visits, conversion rates from registrations to paid actions, repeat conversions, time spent on landing pages, and bounce rates.
Some of the advertiser’s KPIs are undisclosed to prevent fraudulent conversions from prohibited resources.
What happens if a webmaster uses fraud or prohibited traffic?
All traffic generated is not paid. The webmaster’s account is banned/deleted without the possibility of re-registration. Further work with Afflex is considered impossible. Do you have any other questions? Contact your personal manager, who will know the answer.
